Foods to choose more often

These foods support everyday health because they provide fibre, vitamins, minerals, or protein and fit well into balanced daily eating.

Choose more often 🥦

Vegetables

Examples: Broccoli, carrots, spinach, cucumbers, peppers etc.

Why they are good

High in vitamins, minerals, and fibre. Canada’s food guidance recommends eating plenty of vegetables and fruits regularly.

Long term effects

Supports digestion, heart health, and overall disease prevention.

Choose more often 🍎

Fruits

Examples: apples, berries, bananas, and oranges

Why they are good

Provide fibre, vitamins and is a naturally sweet snacks.

Long term effect

Can support heart health and lower risk of chronic disease as part of a balanced diet.

Choose more often 🌾

Whole grains

Examples: Oatmeal, brown rice, whole grain bread, whole wheat pasta

Why are they good

Very high in fibre then refined grains, which helps with fullness and digestion.

Long term effect

May help with blood sugar control, cholesterol, and weight management.

Choose more often 🍳

Protein rich food

Examples: beans, lentils, eggs, fish, chicken, tofu, yogurt, nuts

Why are they good

Canada’s food guide recommends choosing protein foods, especially plant based options.

Long term effect

Maintain muscle and support overall health when prepared in healthier ways.

Food to eat less often

These foods can still appear sometimes, but eating them less often may help lower long term health risks linked to excess sugar, sodium, and saturated fat.

Eat less often 🧋

Sugary drinks

Examples: pop, energy drinks, sweetened juice, bubble tea

Why should be limited

These drinks are often high in sugar and low in nutrients.

Long term effects

May increase risk of weight gain, type 2 diabetes, and dental problems.

Eat less often 🍬

Highly processed snack foods

Examples: chips, candy, instant noodles

Why should be limited

Often high in sodium, sugar or saturated fat.

Long term effects

May cause high blood pressure and weight gain.

Eat less often 🍟

Deep fried foods

Examples: fries, fried chicken, fried fast food

Why should be limited

Often high in calories and saturated fat.

Long term effect

May raise cholesterol and increase risk of heart disease.

Eat less often 🧂

Foods high in salt

Examples: processed meats, fast food, salty snacks

Why should be limited

Too much sodium can raise blood pressure.

Long term effect

Hypertension, heart disease, and stroke.
